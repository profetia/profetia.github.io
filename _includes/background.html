Bio

My research interest spans broadly in computer systems, including high performance computing, operating systems and distributed systems. My research focuses on building high performance and reliable systems.

I obtained my B.E. degree in Computer Science from ShanghaiTech University in July 2025. During my undergraduate years, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Yiting Xia at Max Planck Institute for Informatics, focusing on optical data center networks. I also worked with Profs. Zhice Yang and Zhihao Jiang at ShanghaiTech on multi-core systems scheduling and formal verification for medical diagnosis.

Prior to UIUC, I was a HPC infra engineer at Ubiquant, where I built high-performance storage systems for LLMs. I also led the Geekpie HPC team in ISC'24 and ASC'24. Moreover, I interned as a software engineer at Tencent in 2024. I'm also a Rust enthusiast and a regular contributor to Clippy.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I enjoy swimming, cooking, and traveling.
